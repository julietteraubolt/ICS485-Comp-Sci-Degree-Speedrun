Game Pitch

Experience Comp Sci Degree Speed Run, a relatable game with a classic feel. Work through your ICS degree as you navigate the unique challenges that come with each year. Get your degree by working through each year's minigame and meeting plenty of fun and familiar characters along the way.


Features include:

  • Visual Novel RPG Game with a cartoon aesthetic
  • Four years that reflect the various stages every ICS major experiences
  • Engaging but intuitive minigames to work through along the way

Storyline Overview:

  • Freshman year: jittery ambition meet ICS 111 and Brad the Business Major
  • Sophomore year: welcome to the world of nightclasses and tech bros
  • Junior year: may the algorithms be ever in your favor
  • Senior year: the hardest part of capstone is the senioritis
